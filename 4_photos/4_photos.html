PHOTOS


This section is tied a little bit to the start of my blogs, but in writing up a ton about my life and what I see and do, I realized just how visual of a person I am. Ever since then, I've done my best to flourish that preference, mostly through the simple act of taking a shitton of pictures.

All of those that have gone hiking, walked through a city, climbed up a mountain, or done something else along those lines with me, have noticed I'm usually the one lagging behind the group with my camera pointed at something hoping I can turn that view into something representing art. Somtimes it works, sometimes it doesn't.

As I'm the one controlling this site though, I get to selectively show you only the times where it works! Ownership priveleges, gotta love it!
