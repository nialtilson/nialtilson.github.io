WANDERING INTO THE UNKNOWN WITH NIAL


My five years in Germany have come and gone in the blink of an eye, and after a tumultuous decision to return and getting back to the US, we know's what's coming down the line.

Life is here and full of options, but what the fuck should I do? So many choices and options and things to consider. Where should I live? What do I want to do for work? Am I happy? Am I a good person? What kind of adventures do I want to dive into? Honestly, kinda feeling like life is to be defined and my Analysis Paralysis is hampering the enjoyment and excitement.

Despite whatever reservations, hesitations, and anxiety I have, life is moving forward, and it's time to adventure right into it.
