BLOGS


It all started way back when, in the fateful year 2013. I was a fresh college grad with barely an idea of the world getting ready to move to a foreign country for a year. I had no idea it was going to completely change my life, but that's exactly what it did.

How is that relevant for blogs? Well, that year was when I decided to start documenting (publicly) what the hell is going on in my life. The main reason was to let my family back home know what was going on with me halfway across the world. From there, it's developed into a way for me to keep track of what I've done, reflect on what I've seen and learned, and help me realize just how lucky of a human being I am.

That blog has transformed slightly as I've jumped to a few different phases in my life since then, but I've done my best to try and keep at it. Some months more successfully than others.
