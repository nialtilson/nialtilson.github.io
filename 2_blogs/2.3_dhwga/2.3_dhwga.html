DEUTSCHLAND, HERE WE GO AGAIN!


Now it's time for the next chapter...

Over a year and a half in the works, my life has been uprooted once again and shipped across the world back to Germany. My former boss reached out to me and worked hard to get me back to his team in Germany. It took me a long time to finally commit, as my Family, my Friends, my PNW, all of it is not something easily given up.

But after tons of deliberation and internal debate, I finally pulled the trigger. And with that, my adventures have yet again a European flair all around them. To start, it's a two-year gig, but who knows how things will eventually shake out.

Have no worries though, dear readers. Though my life is now back in Germany, the adventures will continue!
