A CRESCENDO TO THE START


I was told by my boss last week to "enjoy the rest of the summer". All 10 days of it left...

Things are slowly coming to a wrap for my time here in Washington. It is sad. I love Washington, the Pacific Northwest, and all the people and places that I call dear to me here. Not only that, but I have no idea when I will be returning here.

As sad as that is to realize, I am doing my best to maintain my focus on what is to come. I will always have these people to connect with and reunite. Good friends never leave you, no matter how rare you see them or how far away you go. So regardless of what happens down the road, I will always have an amazing group of friends and family cheering me on, no matter where I go.

Quick post. BOOM.
