Article about Millennials and Travel

The Atlantic - How Millenials are Changing International Travel

This article really puts this year and a lot of things going on in my life and with my generation into perspective. The standard goal of going to school, finding a job, and grinding away for 40 years until retirement is slowly eroding away as people see that there is so much more flexibility in life, and coming to the realization that life is meant to be lived, not worked.

Herr Mielke

Never have I ever seen so much attention and love given to a robotic lawn mower. Not just any lawn mower, my landlady's (Frau Mielke) Herr Mielke, as he is endearingly referred to.

He works constantly at keeping the grass in order, and he is a constant topic of conversation. So far, every time we've grilled or sat around in the back yard, Herr Mielke has had a good 5-10 minutes time in our conversations. It's pretty interesting/ridiculous/funny.

And now his home just got a little beautification.

Garden Life

I am unbelievable lucky for everything that I've been able to do and experience this year, and the last 5 months its been centered around the awesome place I've been living.

I have an awesome landlady, and awesome roommates, and I am unbelievably thankful for every aspect of it. As an example, I went out drinking last Saturday night, came back late, and didn't wake up til 11:30 (new sleeping-in record!!! :-D ). As I woke up, I saw a text from Elke asking if I wanted to join her for breakfast.

Well, standing up initiated the hangover, but Elke had everything ready for me. So I just rolled out of bed and was treated to delicious Austrian sausage and bacon on her roof terrace. Let's just say I'm all sorts of lucky.

Green Porno

Lutz was invited to some play for his birthday by Elke, but since he had to work, I was called up from the bench to fill in this role. So I went with Elke and a few of her work friends to this thing called Green Porno, by Isabella Rossellini. Let's just say it was interesting.

I learned a lot about how animals have sex, which was really interesting. For instance, did you know that the female duck has multiple vaginal cavities, so she can have many mates, and essentially decide which one will actually become the father? Crazy.

Well it was definitely a unique, and cultural experience, and quite unique having a 62-year-old lady so vibrant and energetic on the topic of animal sex. I would have never gone to that on my own volition, but am pretty happy that I did, if nothing else for the story, but also for the atmosphere and the fun facts.
