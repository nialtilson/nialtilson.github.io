PROJECTS


Hopefully those that know me would agree. But if not, well, too bad. I consider myself a tinkerer; I like to build, make, fix, all that kinds of stuff. Because of that, I'm commonly finding myself in the midst of a project. As you would expect, most of those projects are riddled by poor definition, drastic project creep, sometimes too deep attention to detail, and god knows what other common failures of personal projects. Despite that, I love it.

In the interest of not trying to hoard everything for myself, and maybe provide some motivation, ideas, tips, or tricks to fellow builders and tinkerers, I figured I might as well try and document that which I build, fix, create, or whatever else my hands end up working with to stay occupied.
